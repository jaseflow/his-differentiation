Sofitel Noosa Pacific Resort places you next to Noosa Beach and within 2 miles (3 km) of Noosa National Park. This 176-room, 5-star hotel has a restaurant, a full-service spa, and an outdoor pool. It's on a river and also near Sunshine Beach Village Shops and Ed Webb Park.

Dining

Enjoy a leisurely bite to eat at Noosa Beach House, the hotel's onsite restaurant. Savor a meal from the comfort of your room with 24-hour room service. Buffet breakfast is offered for a fee each morning from 6:30 AM to 10:30 AM.

Rooms

The 176 air-conditioned accommodations feature kitchenettes and furnished balconies, and there's space to spread out with sitting areas. Guests can expect to find WiFi and 42-inch LCD TVs with satellite channels and pay movies. Beds are dressed in premium bedding and bathrooms offer jetted tubs, hair dryers, and free toiletries. Minibars, coffee makers, and ceiling fans are also standard.

Property features

At Sofitel Noosa Pacific Resort, guests enjoy a full-service spa, an outdoor pool, and a health club. Parking is available for AUD 25.00 per day (AUD 35.00 per day for valet parking), and there's also limo/town car service. The 24-hour front desk has multilingual staff ready to assist with dry cleaning/laundry, concierge services, and luggage storage. Other amenities at this luxury hotel include a spa tub, a sauna, and WiFi in public areas.

Hotel Amenities

Hotel Amenities

In addition to a full-service spa, Sofitel Noosa Pacific Resort features an outdoor pool and a health club. The hotel offers a restaurant. A swim-up bar is on site where guests can unwind with a drink. A computer station is located on site and high-speed wireless Internet access is available for a surcharge.

Business-related amenities at this 5-star property consist of limo/town car service and meeting rooms. Event facilities measuring 8127 square feet (755 square meters) include conference space. This luxury hotel also offers a spa tub, a sauna, and spa services. Parking is available onsite for a surcharge. A total renovation of this property was completed in August 2013.

Sofitel Noosa Pacific Resort is a smoke-free property.

Absolutely brilliant

Friends getaway
by Harriet W from Bedfordshire

Absolutely brilliant

Posted Aug 23, 2016
Arrived at the noosa Sofitel hotel where we were warmly greeted by a senior concierge who arranged for our bags to be taken to our room straight away. Outside was spotless, well lit with water fountains and lights to give a special feel. We then checked in, very warm and friendly staff who seemed genuinely interested in our trip. On arrival to our room, our bag was neatly inside. We were more than impressed, huge modern rooms, really clean and decorated tastefully! Two large double beds with what appeared to be Egyptian cotton bedding, sofa, large screen tv with plenty of channels to peruse. Balcony overlooking the town. Bathroom was spacious, modern and spotless with a luxurious jacuzzi bath. The hotel itself also contains a lovely swimming pool which sadly we didn't have time to try out, but had an in pool swim up bar and jacuzzi. As we were leaving early in the morning, the hotel asked if we would like a breakfast box preparing and delivering to our room which was lovely and on time as promised. The hotel is located perfectly in the centre of noosa strip, but away from any noise from the nearby restaurants. I would HIGHLY recommend to stay here if visiting noosa (we only had one night as were going on a trip to Fraser Island the next day) but wish we had more time. The only slight down side is parking is $25 per day, so $50 for overnight 2 day stay, however we parked in the allocated parking bay opposite the hotel on the roadside free of charge for the 24 hours! Lovely hotel
